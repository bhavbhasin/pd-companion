# Marketing plan

Phased to the launch-checklist gates (docs/app-store-launch-checklist.md) so marketing never runs ahead of the product.

**Positioning:** "The Parkinson's companion that measures, not asks." Passive, continuous, private. Privacy is a feature: subscriptions fund it; data never leaves the device.

| Phase | When | Moves | Metric |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0 · Build audience | Now → Gate A | Email waitlist on kampa.health (decoupled from app data, never symptom-triggered) · LinkedIn cadence (patient/clinician/recruiter) + X (technical) · TestFlight link as CTA | Waitlist size, installs |
| 1 · Quiet listing | Gate A | ASO basics, claims-clean · site CTA flips to App Store · one "it's live" post | Organic installs, reviews |
| 2 · Community launch | Gate B | PD communities as participant (r/Parkinsons, PMD Alliance, Davis Phinney) · clinician channel via visit-prep PDF · PD podcasts/newsletters · Apple editorial pitch | Install→first-insight funnel, D7/D30 |
| 3 · Monetize | Phase 4 paywall | Paid tier for new cohorts, privacy-funding story | Free→paid, churn |

**Constraints:** founder's health details are never part of the story ("built with PD patients"). No health-data-driven marketing or manufactured engagement. Batch website deploys (Netlify credits). X stays technical.
