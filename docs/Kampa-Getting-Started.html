Getting Started
Your first week with Kampa

Kampa watches in the background. Your tremor, sleep, and walking are tracked automatically - no tapping, no daily check-ins. The more context it has - medications, meals, workouts - the sharper its insights get, and almost all of that already lives in Apple Health and your Watch, so there's little new to keep up.

Set up — 3 steps, once

  1. Install Kampa from the TestFlight email invitation, then open it.
  2. Allow both permission screens the first time - Health data (tap Turn On All) and Medications (tap Allow). Tapped the wrong one? Fix it in Settings › Apps › Health › Data Access & Devices › Kampa.
  3. Wear your Apple Watch during the day, and overnight if you track sleep.

Tracked automatically — you never log these

Tremor & dyskinesia from the Apple Watch; walking & gait, sleep, heart rate, and HRV from your iPhone and Watch. Once permissions are on, this happens on its own. Tremor tracking begins after about a day of Watch wear, so an empty tremor view at first is normal — and if your phone still looks empty after that, open Kampa on your Apple Watch for about 30 seconds (iPhone nearby) to sync.

The context that sharpens it — mostly things you already do

Kampa doesn't make you keep a separate logbook - it reads what you already track, so you log the way you normally do:

  • Medications — record your doses in Apple Health › Search › Medications. Kampa reads them automatically - dose timing is one of its most useful insights.
  • Workouts — just start one on your Apple Watch as usual; Kampa picks it up automatically.
  • Food — the one thing you note inside Kampa: tap + on the Review screen and type it plainly ("5 almonds with tea") — no counting.

Reading it

  • Review (home) — your day on one timeline: sleep, tremor, and events (💊 medication, 🍴 food, 🏃 workout). Use the ‹ › arrows to change days.
  • Insights (💡 top-right) — patterns across weeks and months, each with an honest confidence level (Watching → Emerging → Strong) and a "discuss with your neurologist" note where it matters. The longer you use Kampa, the more it can tell you.

Telling us what you think

You're one of the first people to use Kampa, so your reactions matter. Send feedback from TestFlight (Kampa › Send Feedback, screenshots welcome) or just message me. No note is too small - "I didn't understand this screen" is gold.

Requirements: iPhone 11 or later (iOS 26) and Apple Watch Series 4 or later (watchOS 10) - the Watch is how Kampa reads tremor.  Not medical advice. Kampa shows you your own data (n-of-1); it is not a medical device and does not diagnose or treat. Never change a medication or dose based on the app - always go through your neurologist. A calm day or "no clear pattern yet" is real information, not a failure.
