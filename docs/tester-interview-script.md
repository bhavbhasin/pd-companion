# Kampa — Tester Interview Script

Recurring check-in with active testers (John S, +2). Keep the **core** identical every session so answers can be tracked over time; add **one rotating deep-dive** per session.

Technique: ask for the *last specific instance*, not generalizations ("when did you last open it" > "do you find it useful"). Stay silent after asking — let them fill the gap. Never defend the product mid-interview; just capture the complaint. You're collecting data, not selling.

## Core (every session, same wording)

1. **Recency / habit** — When did you last open Kampa, and what made you open it?
   *(Retention signal. Opens-only-when-nudged is a finding.)*
2. **Most-used surface** — Which screen or feature are you on most?
3. **Logging burden** — How much effort does logging take? Have you used the voice/mic input?
4. **Insight payoff** — Has the app told you anything about your own patterns you didn't already know?
5. **Trust** — Did you believe it? Did it match what you feel?
   *(A tester who sees insights but doesn't trust them > a feature request.)*
6. **Missing capability** — What did you go looking for that wasn't there?
7. **Friction / dislike** — What's the most annoying thing about using it?

## Rotating deep-dive (pick one)

- Has anything the app showed you changed a decision, or something you'd raise with your neurologist?
- Walk me through the last time an insight appeared — what did it say, what did you do?
- If Kampa disappeared tomorrow, what would you miss?
